Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dayflow? +

Dayflow is a comprehensive Human Resource Management System (HRMS) designed to simplify HR processes for businesses of all sizes. It combines employee management, attendance tracking, time-off requests, and personal profile management in a single platform.

Who can use this system? +

Dayflow is designed for organizations of all sizes. It supports multiple user roles including employees, managers, HR personnel, and administrators, each with appropriate access levels and permissions.

Is my data secure? +

Absolutely. Dayflow implements enterprise-grade security measures including role-based access control, secure data storage, and encrypted communications. Your sensitive HR data is protected with industry-standard security protocols.

Can employees update their profile? +

Yes, employees can update their personal information, upload documents, manage their resume sections, and view their attendance records. Certain sensitive fields may require administrative approval depending on your organization's policies.

Is salary information private? +

Salary information is strictly controlled with role-based access. Only authorized personnel (HR, Admin, and the employee themselves) can view salary details. All access is logged for security and compliance purposes.
